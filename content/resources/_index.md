## Join Matrix

Matrix is our decentralized chat alternative to Discord.

**Step 1:** Create a free account at [app.element.io](https://app.element.io) (or install Element on mobile)

**Step 2:** Join our community: [#General:matrix.lightfightermanifesto.net](https://matrix.to/#/#General:matrix.lightfightermanifesto.net)

Create your account on **matrix.org** (the default) - not on our server. Federation lets you join from any Matrix server.
