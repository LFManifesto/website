## Features

**Full-Text Search** - Find anything across all content instantly. Press Ctrl+K for quick access.

**Interactive Checklists** - Pre-flight OPSEC, in-flight protocols, landing procedures, spotter duties, and more. Progress saves automatically.

**Calculation Tools** - Payload delivery request generator, mil-relation calculator, RF link margin assessment, and HF antenna calculator.

**Works Offline** - After first load, the entire guide is cached. Access it anywhere without internet.

## Sections

1. **Unmanned Aerial Systems** - UAS protocols, operational considerations, TTPs, payload delivery, counter-UAS techniques, FPV tactics and countermeasures
2. **Communications** - RF fundamentals, antenna systems, DMR operations, HF radio with JS8Call, PACE planning, direction finding awareness
3. **Signals Intelligence** - SDR platforms, signal identification, RF emitter analysis, COMINT and ELINT techniques
4. **Precision Marksmanship** - Shot process, target detection, range estimation, wind reading, rapid engagement, shooter-observer team operations
5. **Mission Planning** - Planning fundamentals, intelligence preparation, aviation operations, communications planning, rehearsals

## Quick Reference Tables

The guide includes formatted tables for:

- UAS frequency bands and protocols
- HF antenna dimensions and propagation distances
- Camouflage patterns by range and conditions
- Wind value by clock position
- Mil-relation target sizes
- SDR hardware comparison
- Ionospheric layers and take-off angles
